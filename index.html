About me

Hello! My name is Duke Ko and I am a personal trainer turned software engineer. I am passionate about discovering ways to make processes more efficient and effective, which is why I find tremendous joy in both coding and physical training. Whether I'm developing a new website or a workout regimen, it is very rewarding to see people benefit from the programs I design.


A few years ago, my passion for sports and my desire to help people drove me to start a new sports league known as TimeOut Sports. I started this league because there was a clear lack in affordable recreational sports leagues in Queens, New York and my desire was to find a way to bring people together through sports. Moving forward, I hope to continue to work towards benefiting my community through any software or applications that I develop.


My love for development really blossomed while working on a project called "COVID Travel Assessment." This project helped me understand how powerful coding can be and how I can have a positive impact through my work.
